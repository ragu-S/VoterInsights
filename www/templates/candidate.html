Martin Abell is an entrepreneur and an environmentalist. He is also a committed community volunteer. Martin is involved as a volunteer with several effective organizations that are making a difference in areas such as housing for our most vulnerable community members, opportunities for youth-at-risk and environmental stewardship and habitat conservation. Martin is a first-generation Canadian, born and raised in downtown Toronto. He and his wife Kim are raising their four young children here. Martin is enthusiastic about the Ontario PC Million Jobs Plan and what it can do for families in Toronto Centre. He believes Ontario can get on the path to better jobs, and wants Toronto Centre to lead the way.
